About Us

What are we you ask?

Five students from APEC schools make up our group. This brand was created as a result of our observations and analyses of the issues affecting teenagers' style, which led us to the conclusion that teens struggle with selecting an outfit, which is why we chose this as our final topic.

Also this topic allows us to utilize our creativity to come up ideas for possible clothing styles for occassions.

Our Content

House of fashion targets teenagers who are having a hard time choosing an outfit.
For this problem we planned our content to publish inspirations and tips for them to follow or even construct their own OOTD(outfit of the day).
With the creativity of our team we can provide our audiences surprises!
